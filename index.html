Rules of the Game

What gets your clip verified.

Only verified clips are paid. Clear rules. No grey areas.

Required

Clear CTA in every clip

Every clip must include a CTA — on-screen text, voiceover, or caption pointing to the brand's link. No CTA = not verified.

Required

Brand style followed

Clips must follow the brand guidelines provided. Tone, framing, messaging must match. Full brief given before you start.

Required

Original edit from source

Each submission must be independently created from the provided source material. Re-uploading another clipper's work disqualifies you.

Required

Organic views only

Only genuine viewer views count. Paid views, bots, or view farms result in permanent account disqualification.

Violation

False or exaggerated claims

Do not make claims the brand hasn't approved — no fake testimonials, fabricated income figures, or misleading statistics.

Violation

Spam distribution

Do not push clips into unrelated groups. Post only where audiences genuinely match the content niche.

Brand-specific terms — restrictions, posting schedule, account requirements — disclosed in full in the WhatsApp community before you commit to any campaign.

Brand Questions

FAQ — For Brands

How does the payment model work exactly? +
You set a marketing budget. 20% goes to ClipGrow as a management/distribution fee. The remaining 80% forms the editor pool — paid to creators based on the CPM you set, until the pool is exhausted. If results don't meet your expectations, you renegotiate the next campaign or walk away. No lock-in.
How do you verify brand guidelines? +
Every clip is manually reviewed before it counts toward payout or reporting. We check: CTA accuracy, brand tone, visual style, no false claims, no off-brand content. Clips that fail are disqualified — the clipper is not paid and the content is flagged. Your brand is always shown in a positive, accurate light.
What is the minimum budget to run a campaign? +
Contact us to discuss — we tailor campaigns based on your product price, target audience, and objectives. We'll recommend a budget that makes mathematical sense for your ROI expectations before you commit.
How do you track sales and traffic? +
You provide a unique tracked link (UTM-tagged or your own tracking tool). Every sale or click that comes through that link is attributed to the campaign. We don't touch your backend — you have full visibility at all times.
What types of products work best? +
Short-form video works best for digital products (courses, SaaS, subscriptions), e-commerce with a clear value proposition, service businesses, and personal brands. If your product solves a real problem for 14–35 year old Indians, it's a great fit for this format.
